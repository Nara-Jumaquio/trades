Trade Guide

TSU (Trade Score Unit): Represents the trade value of items based on rarity, obtainability, and usefulness. No currency is used; all trades are item-for-item only. You can only trade up to two tiers higher (e.g., books → logs → cobblestone). Some items require specific items only.

  • 30 Diamonds = 1 Obsidian
  • 15 Obsidian = 1 Golden Apple
  • 30 Golden Apples = 2 God Apples
